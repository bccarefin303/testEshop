About AAM

Arts Alliance Media (AAM) is the global leader in digital cinema software and services. We offer a wide range of solutions to exhibitors which enable them to reduce costs, increase efficiency, and improve the cinematic experience for their customers. 

Our software touches over 40,000 digital screens globally, while AAM's network operations centre (NOC) supports several thousand screens.
