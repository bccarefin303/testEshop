Why choose SwiftComply?

Kitchens

With SwiftComply you

  • Have fewer kitchen inspections: We’ll keep you on top of your grease-trap management
  • Avoid fines: We’ll send your paperwork to the city inspector to prove your kitchen’s compliance
  • Eliminate paperwork: Keep your waste manifest, invoice and compliance records online
  • Choose trusted service providers: You’ll get a choice of pre-vetted providers
  • Thrive: We focus on your legal obligations giving you more time to focus on your business

Service Providers

With SwiftComply you

  • Grow your business: We’ll market your services to new customers and make them available for booking 24/7
  • Online transactions: We’ll put all your customer transactions online from quotation to job completion
  • Get the recognition you deserve: Your hard work and good business practice is recognized through our regulator approved vetting process
  • Enjoy effortless billing, payment and paperwork: We do your invoicing, facilitate pre-payment on jobs and store all your information digitally

Regulators

SwiftComply partners with regulators to

  • Improve the effectiveness of inspection programs: Use the data from our platform to maximize inspection resources.
  • Reduce administration and paperwork: We digitize paperwork – saving hours of data entry.
  • Improve environmental outcomes: We vet service providers and give kitchens easy access to compliance services.
